Prepare fieldwork for: $ARGUMENTS

1. **research-designer** — produce a study design: case selection (justifiable to a methodologist), periodisation, comparison logic, and a data-collection plan tied to the analysis. Fold in any quantitative-probe priors so fieldwork can *explain* the patterns.
2. **field-planner** — convert the design into an operational manual: itinerary, archive/contact directory (fetch hours and access live), interview protocols with each question keyed to a process-tracing step, and logistics.

The system plans the visit; it cannot take it. Every interview question must trace to an analytical step.
